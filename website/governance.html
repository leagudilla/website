About the Mayor

               

Maria Lourdes R. Pontioso or known as Lovely Reynoso is the current Mayor of City of Tayabas, succeeding his mother Ernida Agpi Reyonoso.

            She was born to a family of public servants. Her father, the late Atty. Leonardo Nadera Reynoso was a Councilor Reynoso for the past eight             years has dedicated former Municipal Councilor while her mother, a former her work to serve her fellow Tayabasins.

            She studied and graduated with honors from and pushed for the passage of several measures and elementary and secondary at Sacred Heart College             and various resolutions and ordinances that redound to the finished her Bachelor of Science in Business Management general welfare of the             Tayabasins through education, at De la Salle University Manila. She worked as a Senior health and sanitation, sports and youth development, Analyst             for Project Finance and Syndicated Loans in a education, tourism, culture and arts, entrepreneurship multinational corporation.

            Mayor Lovely Reynoso, a staunch advocate of “Rise Tayabas”, believes that a progressive City of Tayabascan be achieved in the near future using her             personally crafted legislative governance framework that must be working in synergy with the executive: Healthy Citizens’ Mind (Education) + Healthy Citizens’ Body             (health and sanitation, sports and youth development) + Healthy Local Economy (tourism, culture and arts, entrepreneurship and employment) = Inclusive Growth (Rise Tayabas).
